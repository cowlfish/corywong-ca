Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

This privacy policy explains how Cory Wong, operating as a broker with Trustwell Realty Inc., Brokerage ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, and protects personal information through the website corywong.ca (the "Site").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information through this Site:

  • Email subscription data: When you subscribe to our mailing list, we collect your first name and email address. This data is stored and processed by Kit (ConvertKit), our email service provider. We also record the date and time of your express consent for compliance with Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL).
  • Analytics data: When you consent to cookies, we use Google Analytics (GA4) to collect anonymized usage data including pages visited, time on site, referring URL, browser type, device type, and approximate geographic location. This data is processed by Google LLC.
  • Cookie consent preference: We store your cookie consent choice locally in your browser so we do not ask repeatedly.

2. How We Use Information

  • Email addresses: Used solely to send the newsletter content you subscribed to: real estate, fitness, tech, and other topics. We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Analytics data: Used to understand how visitors interact with the Site, to improve its content and usability.

3. Consent and CASL Compliance

We comply with Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL). We collect express consent before sending commercial electronic messages. When you subscribe:

  • You actively check a consent box (it is not pre-checked)
  • You are clearly informed of who will send the messages and what they will contain
  • Withdraw consent at any time using the unsubscribe link in every email
  • We record the date, time, and method of your consent

4. Cookies

This Site uses cookies only when you provide explicit consent:

  • Google Analytics cookies (_ga, _ga_*): Used to distinguish users and throttle request rate. These cookies are set by Google and persist for up to 2 years.

To withdraw consent, clear your browser's cookies and local storage for this site.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Kit (ConvertKit): Our email service provider. Your name and email address are stored on Kit's servers. See Kit's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics: Collects data transmitted to and processed by Google in the United States. We have configured GA4 to anonymize IP addresses where possible. See Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Your Rights Under PIPEDA

Under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for the collection of your information
  • File a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada

7. Data Retention

Email subscription data is retained for as long as you remain subscribed. When you unsubscribe, your data is removed from our active mailing list. Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted. Cookie consent preferences are stored locally in your browser.

8. Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We update this policy from time to time. Changes are posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
